# 3. Use of AI in the Project

Date: 2026-04-23

## Status

Accepted

## Context

We need to establish guidelines for how Artificial Intelligence (AI) will be integrated and utilized within this project.

## Decision

AI will be used as a collaborative tool to assist with development tasks, code generation, and documentation. However, all AI-generated contributions must be reviewed and approved by a human team member. The ultimate responsibility for any code or documentation produced with the help of AI lies with the person who reviews and merges the changes—not with the AI itself. Every change must be traceable to a real person who has validated its quality and correctness.

## Rationale

Leveraging AI can accelerate development and improve productivity, but human oversight is essential to ensure code quality, maintainability, and accountability. This approach balances the benefits of AI with the need for responsible software engineering practices.

## Consequences

### Positives

- Ensures that no AI-generated code is merged without human validation.
- Enables the use of AI to boost productivity while maintaining quality standards.
- Promotes clear accountability for all changes in the codebase.

### Negatives

- Requires an additional review step for all AI-generated contributions.
- May necessitate two levels of documentation: one for end users and another with more technical details for AI-assisted processes.
